About the Journal

Founded by medical students at the Baylor College of Medicine, the Baylor Medical Student Review (BMSR) is a student-led medical journal created to promote scholarship and education throughout all of medicine. The BMSR serves as a medium for trainees at all levels to share their scholarly work and address critical matters in the medical field through various academic and creative means. In order to foster diverse viewpoints and commentaries, the Journal invites submissions from any healthcare student at the Baylor College of Medicine, as well as medical students, undergraduate students, and trainees at all levels from other schools. The BMSR follows a double-blind peer review process and encourages contributions in original research, reviews, editorials, and letters, in addition to narrative medicine pieces, artwork, and more.
